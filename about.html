About

Why we built Sanket.

Indian retail trading has never had more tips and never had less discipline. Every feed is full of calls. Almost none of them tell you the thing that actually keeps your money safe: how much to risk, and when to walk away.

We started Sanket because we kept watching the same story play out. Smart, capable people, often busy professionals with real jobs, would take a position on a tip, with no defined exit, no sense of how much of their capital was on the line, and no plan for when it went wrong. The idea was rarely the problem. The absence of risk management was.

So we built the opposite of a tip feed. Sanket is a risk-first decision layer. It looks at a stock through three lenses, the health of the business, the technical setup, and the state of the wider market, and turns that into one honest call with the risk already worked out: a defined entry, a target, a stop, and the exact number of shares to buy for your capital.

What we believe

  • A calm decision beats a hot tip. Discipline, repeated, compounds. Excitement, repeated, does not.
  • Risk comes first, not last. The size and the exit are decided before the idea is even worth looking at.
  • Honesty over hype. No guaranteed returns, no cherry-picked screenshots. A conviction score is allowed to say "not strong."
  • Clarity over noise. One clear answer is worth more than ten dashboards to a person with a life to live.

Where we are headed

We are early, and intentionally so. Right now we are working with our first hundred users to get the product genuinely right, and putting the proper regulatory foundation in place before we ever charge for it. The longer ambition is simple: a generation of Indian investors who trade with the calm and discipline of a professional risk desk, whatever the size of their account.

If that is the kind of trading you want for yourself, you are exactly who we are building for.
