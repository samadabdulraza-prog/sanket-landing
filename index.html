Signal in focus

One strategy,
working in the open.

The swing setup above is Sanket's most consistent edge: a quality business, caught on a pullback, with risk defined before you enter. Here is why it holds up, not as a promise, but as a process.

01

Quality filter first

The setup only fires on businesses with strong fundamentals. You are never chasing a technical bounce on a weak company.

02

Buy the pullback, not the hype

It waits for price to fall into an oversold zone near support. You enter where others panic, not where everyone is already crowded in.

03

Momentum must confirm

MACD has to be turning up before the call goes high-conviction. Cheap is not enough; the turn has to be starting.

04

Every signal ships with an entry, a stop, a target and a position size based on your risk per trade. The downside is known before the upside is hoped for.
