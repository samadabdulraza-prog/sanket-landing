The gap

Most apps help you find stocks. Almost none help you manage the risk.

Finding something to buy is the easy part. The part that actually decides whether you keep your money is how much you risk, how big the position is, and when you get out. That is where most retail traders get hurt, and that is exactly where Sanket begins.

The core

Risk management, built into every call.

⚖️

Position sizing that respects your capital

Exactly how many shares, capped to what you can actually afford, so a single stop-out can never blow a hole in your account. Risk a fixed, small slice of capital on every trade, automatically.

🎯

An exit before you enter

Every idea comes with a defined entry, target, and stop, and the reward-to-risk spelled out. No more deciding where to get out after the trade is already against you.

📊

Conviction you can read at a glance

One score that blends business health, the technical setup, and the market regime, so you know how much to trust an idea before you commit a rupee to it.

💧

Liquidity-aware by default

It only surfaces stocks you can actually trade size in, so the plan survives contact with the real market instead of falling apart on a thin order book.

How it works

Three checks. One decision.

01

Is the business sound?

Financial health first. Strong fundamentals under the chart, not just a pretty pattern.

02

Is the setup right?

The technical picture: momentum, trend, and where price sits relative to its range.

03

Is the market on your side?

The wider regime. Even good setups fail when the whole market is fighting you.

The result: a clear call, and a position size you can act on.
The approach

Disciplined by design. Quietly rigorous under the hood.

We will not pretend there is no method, and we will not hand you the recipe. The exact scoring, the weightings, and the data engineering behind a Sanket call are proprietary. What we will tell you is the discipline they all serve.

01

Rules, not moods. Every call follows a fixed, repeatable process. No gut feel, no hot takes, no chasing whatever is trending that morning.

02

Tested before trusted. The logic is checked against history rather than hand-tuned to look good on one chart. Discipline beats a lucky example.

03

Risk is the first input, not an afterthought. Size and exit are decided before the idea is ever shown to you, never bolted on later.

04

Honest about uncertainty. A conviction score is a confidence level, not a promise. Low conviction is allowed to say so, plainly.

Questions

The things people ask first.

Is Sanket investment advice?

No. Sanket provides educational technical analysis and risk-management tools. It is not investment advice, not a recommendation to buy or sell, and we are not SEBI-registered. You make your own decisions, and we encourage you to consult a SEBI-registered advisor before investing. See our Disclosures.

Which markets and stocks does it cover?

Indian equities listed on the exchanges, focused on liquid names you can actually trade size in. Sanket deliberately leaves out the thinly traded long tail, because a plan you cannot execute is not a plan.

Does Sanket place trades or handle my money?

Never. Sanket does not connect to your broker account at all. You sign in with Google, Sanket shows you the plan and the exact order to enter, and you place it yourself in your own broker. We never see or touch your money or your holdings.

What does the conviction score actually mean?

It is a confidence level, blending business health, the technical setup, and the market environment into one number. Higher means the signals agree more strongly. It is never a guarantee of a result.

What will it cost?

We are in an early, invite-only phase and gathering our first hundred users. Pricing comes later, alongside the right regulatory structure. For now, the focus is on getting the product right.

Who is it really for?

Busy professionals who want to trade with discipline but cannot watch screens all day, and who would rather have one clear, risk-managed decision than a feed full of tips.
